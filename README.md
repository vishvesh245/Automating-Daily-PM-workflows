# 🤖 PM-Workflow-Agents

A collection of high-precision **AI System Prompts** designed to automate and elevate the daily workflows of a Senior Product Manager. 

Unlike generic chatbots that simply provide answers, these agents act as **Thinking Partners**. They are built with **gated processes**—forcing the AI to analyze context silently, ask clarifying questions, and pressure-test assumptions before generating a single line of output.

---

## 🚀 Why This Exists
In high-velocity environments like **Quick Commerce**, repetitive tasks—writing PRDs, summarizing meetings, or reviewing technical specs—can become bottlenecks. Standard AI tools often provide generic "filler" content that lacks the nuance of a specific business model.

**This suite is customized to:**
* **Eliminate Repetitive Toil:** Automate the structure so you can focus on the strategy.
* **Enforce Rigorous Thinking:** The agents are programmed to "stop and ask" rather than "guess and proceed".
* **Deep Context Awareness:** Tailored for the UAE market, focusing on metrics like basket size, conversion funnels, and last-mile operations.

## 🤖 Agent Directory

### 1. The Meeting Analyst (`Meeting agent v1.md`)
Turns raw transcripts or notes into structured executive summaries. It flags ambiguous action items with a ⚠️ and highlights specific business risks.
* **Output:** TL;DR, Key Decisions, Action Item Tracker, and Risk Assessment.

### 2. The Technical Reviewer (`Enginner.md`)
Acts as a Senior Engineer (10+ years experience) to pressure-test your product specs.
* **Focus:** Technical feasibility, implementation complexity (LOE), and performance bottlenecks before they hit development.

### 3. The Design System Specialist (`Designer.md`)
A Senior UI/UX Designer expert in mobile-first, quick-commerce flows. It uses a **4-step gated process** and strictly follows the internal Figma design system.
* **Unique Feature:** It stops and asks for a decision if a required component doesn't exist in the library.

### 4. The Brief & PRD Architects (`PRD Brief.md` & `Final PRD.md`)
These agents transform half-formed ideas into "sharp, honest" product briefs and full-scale PRDs.
* **The Socratic Method:** They use Socratic pressure-testing to find edge cases in your logic.
* **Outcome Focused:** They rewrite solution-framed requirements into user outcomes (e.g., changing "Add a button" to "Allow users to quickly reorder").

## 🛠️ How to Use
1. **Select your Agent:** Open the `.md` file for the task at hand.
2. **Initialize:** Copy the content into your LLM of choice (Claude, ChatGPT, etc.) as a **System Prompt**.
3. **Collaborate:** Follow the agent's gated steps. Answer its questions to move from "Intake" to "Final Output."

## 📉 Impact
By using these agents, you ensure:
* **Consistency:** Every PRD and Brief follows the same high standard.
* **Discovery:** You'll find edge cases during the prompt phase, not during the QA phase.
* **Speed:** Go from a rough idea to a dev-ready PRD in a fraction of the time.
